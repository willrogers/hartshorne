Events

The hall and kitchen range is usually open for Heritage Open Days in mid-September. Costumed guides welcome you and show you round the whole of the house.

We are also happy to show round private groups by prior arrangement. There is no charge but we ask for a donation to a Trussell Trust food bank. Please contact Catherine Rogers.
